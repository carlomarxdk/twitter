Vacabulary Analysis

Disclaimer: For out vocabulary analysis, we focus on english tweets only.

Our dataset contains 9 years of troll tweets. In the first few years, less than 50 tweets was published by these accounts every month. Since July 2014, the monthly amount of tweets increased rapidly to tens and even hundreds of thousends. Therefore, our analysis focuses mostly on the tweets published between January 2014 and May 2018.

Tweet Tokenizing

In order to get build the vocabulary of the tweets, we process each tweet separately to remove all twitter handles (retweets, references to other accounts), URLs, hashtags, and emojis. The processed tweet is then tokenized and cleaned from stopwords, digits as well as currencies, and all words with less than 3 characters. Finally, the tokens are normalized to lower case and lemmatized. For each tweet, we return a set of distinct tokens.

TF-IDF

To calculate the TF-IDF for the each word from our final vocabulary, we grouped the tweets into monthly bins. We then calculated the TF-IDF for each month separately. The results, represented by wordclouds are described in the following section.
